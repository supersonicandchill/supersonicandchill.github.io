About the App

Fire Drill Wheel is a shift drill randomizer for fire crews — 115 drills across three modes, built by a 20-year career firefighter.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Tap the "Restore Purchase" button on the Customization screen. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I add my own drills?
Yes — the Customization unlock lets you add, edit, and delete drills from the library.

The wheel isn't spinning. What do I do?
Force-close the app and relaunch. If the issue persists, email us at the address above.
