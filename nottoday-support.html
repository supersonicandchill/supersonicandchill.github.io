Your data

Not Today stores everything locally on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and nothing is collected or shared. Use Settings → Back up my streaks before deleting the app if you want to keep your history.

Common questions

I lost my streak when I reinstalled.
Data is local-only, so uninstalling erases it. Back up first from Settings.

How do reminders work?
Evening and morning reminders are optional, off by default, and only notify about habits you haven't confirmed yet — set in Settings.

Can I track more than one habit?
Yes — pick from the preset list or add your own under "Choose what to track."
